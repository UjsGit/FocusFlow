Privacy Policy for FocusFlow

Effective Date: October 2023

The "No-Data" Guarantee: FocusFlow does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information, browsing history, or user data to external servers.

Data Usage

All focus statistics (peek counts and focus scores) are stored locally on your browser's internal storage. We only access YouTube video titles and durations to provide the snapshot features in the extension popup.

Third Parties

We do not share any data with third parties. No analytics or tracking scripts are used.
